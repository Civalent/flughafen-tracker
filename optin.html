About Flughafen PM

Flughafen PM LLC is a construction project-management company based in Dallas, Texas. We operate an internal time-tracking application that our field employees use to clock in and out of job sites.

What messages we send

This SMS program sends work shift reminders — such as clock-in and clock-out reminders — to our own employees who use the Flughafen PM time-tracking app. Messages are transactional and work-related. We do not send marketing or promotional texts.

Example message

Flughafen PM — Hi Luis, time to clock in. Reply STOP to opt out.

How employees opt in

Only Flughafen PM employees receive these messages. An employee opts in by providing their mobile number to their manager and agreeing to receive shift reminders as part of their employment. Consent is never bought, sold, or shared with third parties.

Message frequency, rates, opt-out

  • Frequency: up to about 2 messages per employee per workday.
  • Rates: message and data rates may apply.
  • Opt out: reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages.
  • Help: reply HELP or contact us at architectluisg@gmail.com.

Privacy

Phone numbers are used solely to deliver work shift reminders to our employees. We do not sell or share mobile information with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.
