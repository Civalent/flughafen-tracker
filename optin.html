Employee shift-reminder SMS program

As part of our internal time-tracking system, Flughafen PM sends work shift reminders — such as clock-in and clock-out reminders — by text message to our own employees. These are transactional, work-related messages. We do not send marketing or promotional texts, and this program is not open to the general public.

Sample message

Flughafen PM — Hi Luis, time to clock in. Reply STOP to opt out.

How employees opt in — verbal consent

Only Flughafen PM employees receive these messages. Consent is collected verbally during hiring/onboarding, when a manager reads the following script and the employee agrees and provides their mobile number:

Verbal consent script (read by the manager to each employee):

“As part of your job at Flughafen PM, we send work shift reminders by text message — for example, reminders to clock in and clock out. May we send these text reminders to your mobile number?

These are work-related messages only, usually no more than two per workday. Message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP at any time to stop receiving them, or reply HELP for help.

Do you agree to receive these text reminders at this number?”

The employee verbally confirms “yes,” provides their mobile number, and the manager records the employee’s name, number, and the date consent was given.

Message frequency, rates & opt-out

  • Frequency: up to about 2 messages per employee per workday.
  • Rates: message and data rates may apply.
  • Opt out: reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages.
  • Help: reply HELP, or contact us at architectluisg@gmail.com.

Privacy

Mobile numbers are used solely to deliver work shift reminders to our employees. We do not sell, rent, or share mobile information or consent with third parties or affiliates for any marketing purpose. No mobile opt-in data is shared with third parties.
